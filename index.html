Hi there, I'm Daniele!

Daniele Grasso is an Italian Data - Driven Journalist based in Madrid. He created and now coordinates the Data Journalism Unit of the Spanish leading digital newspaper, El Confidencial.

Besides its newsroom work, he is also a member of the first pan-European data-journalism projects

GenerationE
The Migrants Files

He developed investigative journalism skills covering a leading role in the Spanish chapters of cross-border investigations 'Lux Leaks' and 'Swiss Leaks', a global effort to reveal the secret accounts of the HSBC.
